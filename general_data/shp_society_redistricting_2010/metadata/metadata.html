Section 1 Overview
Originator Minnesota Legislative Coordinating Commission - Geographic Information Services Office (LCC- GIS)
Title Redistricting Data, Minnesota, 2010
Abstract This data set provides the entire data hierarchy used for the 2010 Redistricting efforts in Minnesota. Includes census block, voting districts, minor civil divisions and counties. Includes MN Secretary of State election data from 2002-2010 that was disaggregated to census block level and aggregated to voting districts, minor civil divisions and counties.
Purpose To provide the data associated with the 2010 Redistricting efforts for geographical research and analysis.
Time Period of Content Date 2010
Currentness Reference Contains files used for the 2010 Redistricting efforts and is complete as of its release. The data will become outdated as of 2020 Redistricting efforts.

Section 2 Data Quality
Attribute Accuracy Attribute data was provided by the Census Bureau and MN Secretary of State
Logical Consistency None
Completeness Complete as of its release 2010
Horizontal Positional Accuracy No assessment completed
Vertical Positional Accuracy No assessment completed.
Lineage The data was derived from the 2010 Census TIGER/Line Files.The attributes associated with the data sets represent census demographic information as well MN Secretary of State election data. Certain attribute fields were added to calculate percentages based on census and election data. MN Secretary of State election data from 2002-2010 was disaggregated to census block level and aggregated to voting districts, minor civil divisions and counites. Geographical analysis was done in Maptitude 6.0.
